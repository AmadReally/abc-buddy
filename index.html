🎯 About ABC Buddy

ABC Buddy is designed for children ages 4-7 who are learning the alphabet. Each interaction is carefully crafted to be age-appropriate, encouraging, and educational.

🛡️ Safety First

All content is child-safe. No external links, no advertisements, no data collection. Just pure learning fun!

📖 Learning Goals

  • Letter recognition (A-Z)
  • Letter sounds (phonics)
  • Letter-word associations
  • Fine motor skills (tracing)
  • Memory and matching

💡 Tips

  • Let your child explore at their own pace
  • Celebrate every attempt, not just correct answers
  • Try the sing-along section together
  • Limit screen time to 15-20 minute sessions
